You can request deletion of your data associated with the D24 Studio WhatsApp Bot at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at info@d24.studio
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request – WhatsApp Bot
  3. Include:
    • Your WhatsApp phone number (in international format)
    • A brief request to delete your data

What Happens Next

  • We may ask you to verify ownership of the phone number.
  • Once verified, we will delete or anonymize data unless we must retain it for legal or security reasons.
