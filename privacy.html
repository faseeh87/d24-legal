D24 Studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the D24 Studio WhatsApp Bot (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • WhatsApp account information: such as your phone number and basic WhatsApp identifiers required to deliver messages.
  • Message content you send: text and other content you choose to message to the bot.
  • Usage and log data: timestamps, delivery status, error logs, and basic interaction metadata to keep the Service reliable.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service (reply to messages, deliver features you request).
  • To troubleshoot, secure, and improve performance (detect errors, prevent abuse/spam).
  • To comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms.

Legal Bases (where applicable)

  • Legitimate interests: operating and securing the Service.
  • Consent: where required, based on your interaction and settings.
  • Legal obligations: complying with valid legal requests.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers: hosting, logging, and infrastructure vendors that process data on our behalf to run the Service.
  • Compliance and safety: if required by law or to protect users, our rights, or the public.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to operate the Service, resolve issues, and meet legal requirements. We may delete or anonymize data sooner when it is no longer needed.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to reduce risk.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can stop using the Service anytime by discontinuing WhatsApp messages to the bot.
  • You may request access or deletion of your data by emailing us.
